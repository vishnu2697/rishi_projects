Open positions

UI/UX Designer

Immediate Join

Bangalore, India

Requirements

We are looking for a talented UI Designer to create amazing user experiences. The ideal candidate should have an eye for clean and artful design, possess superior UI skills and be able to translate high- level requirements into Interaction flows and artifacts, and transform them into beautiful, intuitive, and functional user interfaces.

1. Collaborate with product management and engineering to define and implement Innovative solutions for the product direction, visuals, and experience.
2. Execute all visual design stages from concept to final hand-off to engineering.
3. Conceptualize original ideas that bring simplicity and user-friendliness to complex design roadblocks.

Required Candidate profile

Candidate with more than 2 Years of Working Experience as a Web/UI Designer is preferable.

1. Proven UI experience.
2. Demonstrable UI design skills with a strong portfolio.
3. Experience in creating wireframes, user flows, process flows and site maps.
4. Proficiency in Photoshop, Illustrator, or other visual design and wireframing tools.
5. Good understanding of Apple's human interface design guidelines, Android design and other mobile platform designs, including Google's material design.
6. Knowledge of HTML, CSS, Javascript will be a big plus
7. Excellent visual design skills with sensitivity to user-system interaction
Role Graphic/Web Designer

Education

UG : Graduation Not Required, Any Graduate in Any Specialization
PG : Post Graduation Not Required, Any Postgraduate in Any Specialization
Doctorate : Doctorate Not Required
